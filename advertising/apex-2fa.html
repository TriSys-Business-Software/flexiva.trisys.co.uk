You can now add additional security to your TriSys account by enabling two factor authentication (2FA).

This works by utilising your mobile phone as an additional level of security, effectively preventing access to your account to anyone who is not in possession of your mobile phone.

When you enable 2FA, your TriSys login is synchronised with your authenticator app on your mobile phone. Every time you login, TriSys will prompt you for a six digit code which is displayed on your mobile phone. This code refreshes every thirty seconds, thus making it impossible for an intruder to guess your current code.

Please get in touch if you wish to learn more about two factor authentication.
