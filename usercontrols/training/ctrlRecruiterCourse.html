This training course is designed specifically for recruitment consultants.

The course consists of a series of videos broken down into categories comprising a group of distinct videos describing and demonstrating important application functionality. You may find it useful to tear off a browser tab onto a separate monitor and run the training course leaving you free to experiment with the application whilst you learn.

It is recommended that you watch the videos in order during the course, however you will be able to revisit this course at any time and watch any video as desired. It is also recommended that you have a computer with a fast internet connection and sound enabled so that you can enjoy high quality video broadcasting accompanied by the presenters voice.

The course programme is as follows:

  1. Introduction
    1. What is TriSys?
      This is an overview of the TriSys recruitment software system and the problems it addresses specifically for recruitment agencies.
    2. The Big Picture
      This breaks down the numerous components and shows the flow of data throughout the system.
  2. Basics
    1. Free Trial
      How to connect to Apex and sign-up for the free trial, as well as subscribe to your own dedicated recruitment database.
    2. Logging In
      Using the most convenient TriSys application on your devices, and ensuring quick and secure login.
    3. Welcome Wizard
      Completing essential user settings after signing up.
    4. Navigation
      How to find your way around the application.
    5. Tasks
      Explains how notes/history and scheduled tasks helps you be more productive.
    6. Dashboard
      Centralised reporting and analytics showing key preformance indicators.
    7. Data Grid
      How to make the most of the data grid which is at the heart of the application.
  3. Looking up Records
    1. Contacts
      Quickly find contacts and open their records for viewing or editing.
    2. Companies
      Quickly find companies and open their records for viewing or editing.
    3. Requirements
      Quickly find requirements/vacancies and open their records for viewing or editing.
    4. Placements
      Quickly find placements and open their records for viewing or editing.
    5. Saved Searches
      Create re-usable searches which can be easily used in business workflow to quickly and efficiently retrieve records.
    6. Boolean Searching
      How to use boolean searching to create sophisticated searches using grouping and AND, OR operators.
  4. Data Entry & Forms
    1. Contact
      Viewing and edit contact details in a form.
    2. Job Title
      Adding and looking up job titles.
    3. Company
      Viewing and edit company details in a form.
    4. Requirements
      Viewing and edit requirement/vacancy details in a form.
    5. Placement
      Viewing and edit placement details in a form.
    6. Diary
      Managing your scheduled tasks using the diary manager form.
  5. Actions
    1. Actions Overview
      Explains what actions are, how they work and what business benefits they bring.
    2. E-Mail Marketing
      Using actions to promote and market your recruitment business through e-mail.
    3. Recruitment Workflow
      Using actions with candidate resourcing tools to progress candidates through sending CV's, interviews, offers, rejections to successful placement.
    4. Mail-Merging
      Build your own templates to merge fields from your database into e-mail and letters.
  6. Importing Data
    1. CV Auto-Recognition
      Automate the recognition of CV/Resume data from e-mail or files to create candidate records.
    2. Formatting CV's
      Generate multiple formatted CV's from original to send to clients.
    3. Converting PDF to .DOCX
      Convert candidate supplied Adobe Acrobat portable document files into more usable Microsoft Word files.
    4. Outlook Integration
      Import clients and candidates from e-mail.
    5. LinkedIn
      The browser extension which works to synchronise your LinkedIn contacts with your recruitment database.
  7. Miscellaneous
    1. Post-It Notes
      Send electronic yellow notes to colleagues such as taking a phone message, or reminders.
    2. Chat
      Have a text and file dialogue with colleagues and link to database records such as contacts, companies and jobs.
    3. User Options
      Configure the application to get the best experience and performance.
    4. Getting Support
      How to get technical support or speak to your account manager.
