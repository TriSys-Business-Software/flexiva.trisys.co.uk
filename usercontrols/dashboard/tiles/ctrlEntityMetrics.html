Contacts


Contacts are people whose records are held in your database. A contact can be a client or a candidate or a user. A client contact is someone with whom you do business by placing candidate contacts. You and your recruitment agency colleagues are user contacts.

Companies


Each company record may have multiple addresses with each client contact working at each address. All business of placing candidates us conducted between your recruitment agency and the client company.

Requirements


Requirements are vacancies in which your clients request the placement of one or more candidates. Each requirement is linked to a client contact and company and owned by a recruitment agency consultant. Sophisticated workflow exists within the requirement to advertise to, search and shortlist candidates.

Placements


Placements are filled requirements in which a candidate has been successfully placed at your client company. Each placement is linked to a client contact and company and candidate and owned by a recruitment agency consultant.
