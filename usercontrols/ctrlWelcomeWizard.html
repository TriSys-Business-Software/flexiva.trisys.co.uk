Your Profile

When you generate correspondence using the actions workflow, your personal details are automatically merged into outbound communications such as e-mails. By setting these essential profile properties, your external communications will look polished and professional.


Company Profile

When you generate correspondence using the actions workflow, your company details are automatically merged into outbound communications such as e-mails. By setting these essential company properties, your external communications will look polished and professional.


New Job Prefixes

When you create new job opportunities they are known to candidates as vacancies, but are typically called requirements within TriSys. When you place candidates, these are typically called placements within TriSys. Both new requirements and placements are given an automatically generated reference number. In order to easily identify your specific vacancies when advertised on social media, or job boards, you can prefix this reference number with your initials or nick name or any other string.

Automated E-Mail

TriSys allows mass e-mailing directly from the application without using Outlook. The TriSys online candidate, client portals and mobile products also have automated e-mail generation capabilities. When these automated e-mails are delivered, they must come from your own simple mail transfer protocol (SMTP) server. Typically this is hosted by your e-mail provider and you should have been supplied with your credentials. Set these below to allow automated e-mails to be sent from your account.
For Microsoft accounts, use smtp.office365.com as the server address, and for Google Mail accounts, use smtp.gmail.com as the server address.

Social Networks

Your active vacancies can be posted to both social networks and job boards. We utilise the free Buffer (buffer.com) service to post to multiple social networks. So please sign up and enter your details below. You should also enter the address of your social network pages so that TriSys can navigate and show you job posts automatically.
