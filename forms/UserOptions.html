Show Help

The Show Help reminder can be shown by selecting this check box. When turned on, a popup window will open after login to remind you that the Show Help can be invoked from anywhere in the application by pressing the F1 key, or by clicking the Show Help button on the toolbar.
The artificial intelligence (AI) chatbot can be turned on at any time by selecting this check box. When turned on, a chat button will be visible at the bottom right of the screen. By clicking this button, you can ask the chatbot questions about this application which has been trained on the user guide and help documentation.

Country/Language

You can override your current country and language settings when you are visiting another country or wish to test your own customised help, forms or messages.
