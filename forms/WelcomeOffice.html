Office Add-In
Summary of the main features of the TriSys Add-In for Microsoft Office.

Features

CRM Database
Connected to your recruitment customer relationship management database.
Pull data into Office, and push data into TriSys.

Lookup Contacts
Lookup all your clients, candidates and companies.
Automatically view contacts when selecting e-mail messages.

Contact Summary
Summary of each contact displayed.
Quick access to phone numbers, e-mail addresses etc..

CV Viewers
Quickly view CV's of candidates.
Multiple CV's and multiple types of document

Requirements/Vacancies
Access to your live vacancies and shortlisted candidates.

Placements
Access to your candidate placements.
