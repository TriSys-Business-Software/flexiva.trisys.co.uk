Chat Application

This form alows you to conduct real-time recorded chat conversations with your consultant colleagues by embedding rich content such as images, files, formatted text, and most importantly, links to database records i.e. contacts, companies, requirements and placements. Updates to your messages are immediately updated on the recipients computer. If the recipient is not available, or not logged in, your messages are queued so that they can be read at any time.

Please scroll and select your colleague from the list above. Only those consultants who have their photo assigned to their user contact record will be visible in this list. You are able to add and remove your favourite consultants to/from this list using the toolbar.

The chat history will be displayed below the selected consultant.

You can type a message and use the text formatting hover toolbar to set colour, font, size, bold, italic, underline and strikethrough.

You can use the paperclip icon to attach files.

Press the Send button to send the message to your colleague.

You can edit or delete your messages using the cogwheel button, and remove your messages from the toolbar.
